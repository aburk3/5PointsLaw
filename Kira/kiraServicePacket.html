Service Packets

SERVICE: Within 3 days of filing the Complaint and paying the filing fees, the complaint packets have to be served to the Defendant. Each court has a different preference for how they want documents to be assembled and served.



Northern District


  • Complaint Documents
    • One Court-Stamped copy of Complaint per Defendant with Exhibits
    • One Court-Stamped copy of Corporate/Conflict Disclosure Statement per Defendant
    • Two copies of the Summons per Defendant
      • Be sure to put the case number on the Summons (You can write this directly onto the paper)
    • NO Request for Service needed
  • Certified Mailing - One Envelope per Defendant - DO NOT SEAL THE ENVELOPE -- The Court will do this part
    • Print a certified mail card for each Defendant needing to be served. The mail-to address is the same on listed at the end of the Complaint
    • Ensure that the return address is the Clerk's Office:
      • Sharon N. Harris, Clerk of Court
      • U.S. District Clerk of Court
      • Northern District of Alabama
      • 1579 5th Avenue North
      • Birmingham, AL 35203
        •
    • Click here to view an example of a Certified Mailing Label for the Northern District
    • Write the case number above the bar on the front of the green return card and under the address on the green void receipt

Middle District


  • Complaint Documents - Be sure to make a packet for each Defendant
    • Original Complaint with Exhibits
    • Original Request for Service per Defendant with Attorney's Signatures
    • Original Corporate/Conflict Disclosure Statement per Defendant with Attorney's signature
    • 2 copies of Summons per Defendant
  • Certified Mailing - One Envelope per Defendant - DO NOT SEAL THE ENVELOPE - The Court will do this part
    • Print a certified mail card for each Defendant needing to be served. The mail-to address is the same on listed at the end of the Complaint
    • Ensure that the return address is the Clerk's Office:
      • Debra P. Hackett, Clerk of Court
      • U.S. District Clerk of Court
      • Middle District of Alabama
      • One Church Street
