Complaint Styling

PROOFREAD COMPLAINT PACKET: Per Attorney's instruction, be sure to proof Complaint for any grammatical or formatting errors. If there are any issues with the substance of the Complaint, confirm with Attorney prior to saving any changes.



Proofing and Formatting


  • Style (top section of document)
    • Ensure that the appropriate District and Division are listed
    • Ensure that the Plaintiff and Defendant names are correct. Check the Secretary of State's Website for the correct name of the Defendant.
    • Check Secretary of State for Registered Agent or Proper Address
    • Civil Action Number - For new cases, this should read "Jury Trial Requested" unless otherwise instructed.
  • Body
    • Ensure that all numbers and bullets are in correct order
    • Proof for any grammatical or formatting errors
    • Ensure that the Defense Counsel is correct, if necessary. We may not know who it is yet.
    • Ensure that the appropriate pronouns are used for both the Plaintiff and Defendant
    • Write the case number above the bar on the front of the green return card and under the address on the green void receipt
  • Closing
    • Ensure that the correct attorney is used for both the Plaintiff and Defendant (if applicable)
  • Exhibits
    • Create Cover sheets for Exhibits utilizing the same style as the Complaint
    • Ensure that Exhibits are appropriately redacted of all personal information for the client (Date of Birth, Phone Number, Address, Social Security Number, etc.)
