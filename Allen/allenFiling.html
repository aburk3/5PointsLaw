Middle District


  • File new Complaints by mailing the ORIGINAL DOCUMENTS in a 10x13 envelope to the Clerk's Office.
  • Certified Mail Label (Large Green Label) affixed to a 9x12 envelope with the Defendant's service address (found at the end of the Complaint) as the delivery address and the Clerk's Office as the return address.

  • Debra Hackett, Clerk of Court
  • U.S. District Clerk of Court
  • Middle District of Alabama
